Cardfight Vanguard @ SuperStar Card Gaming

Stand Up, Vanguard!

What's to know?

Cardfight Vanguard is one of SuperStar's most popular card games. Officially sanctioned by Bushiroad, SuperStar sells official Cardfight Vanguard products, including preconstructed decks, play-mats, booster packs/boxes and singles.

Cardfight Vanguard tournaments are held each Wednesday at 6pm, and Saturday at 12pm. Entry fee is just $5, and the prizes range from boosters, to play-mats, and even store credit!
